About Me

I'm Nathan Brown-Bennett, a cybersecurity and software professional with an MSc in Network and Information Security from Kingston University London. I build secure, user-friendly technology for social impact and practical business operations.

With a BSc in Cyber Security & Digital Forensics and experience across security engineering, technical education and product delivery, I combine academic rigour with hands-on development. My work focuses on solutions that are technically robust and genuinely useful.
